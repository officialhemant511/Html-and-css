BGMI TOURNAMENT

Last month, our university's Technical TechGeek Club hosted an epic BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) tournament that saw intense competition between squads from across campus. Over 50 teams registered to battle it out in this action-packed event held right here in our college auditorium. After qualifiers and playoffs that spanned 2 weekends, the final 4 squads gave it their all to emerge victorious in the Grand Finals featuring Sanhok maps. Congratulations to team Avengers for winning the championship trophy and RS 5000 cash prize. Stay tuned for more details and highlights from this landmark gaming event organized by the TechGeek Club! Registration will open soon for the next tournament - bigger, better and more exciting!
